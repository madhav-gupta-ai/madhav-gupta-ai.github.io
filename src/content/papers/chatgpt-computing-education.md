Analyzed real-world student use of ChatGPT in computing education, identifying
three recurring experiences — productive assistance, partial success, and
AI-induced frustration.

Conducted structured interviews with students and faculty and proposed best
practices for AI-assisted learning that emphasize human oversight and structured
prompting.
