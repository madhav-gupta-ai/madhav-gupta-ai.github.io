Extended prior work into a Physics-Informed Neural Network (PINN) framework
modeling ODE systems of control and state vectors for dynamic optimization,
achieving new best values for the batch-reactor and tubular-reactor problems
(0.6109 and 0.5748) with negligible physics loss.

Accepted 2025, in press.
