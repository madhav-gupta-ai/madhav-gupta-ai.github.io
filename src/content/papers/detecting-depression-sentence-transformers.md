Built depression-detection models using word embeddings (GloVe) and sentence
transformers (T5, MiniLM, MPNet), raising the best F1-score from 93.24% to 98.04%
on a curated dataset.

Feature extraction and classification were optimized via Bayesian Optimization
(Optuna) and multi-classifier ensembles. The paper also discusses the ethics of
ML-based depression detection, AI-human collaboration, and psychiatric
applications.
