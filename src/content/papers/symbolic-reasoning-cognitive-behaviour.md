A historical review of Human Cognitive Behaviour Analysis, mapping the shift from
1960s symbolic reasoning to 1990s data-driven learning.

Analyzed seminal systems (ELIZA, PARRY; MYCIN, Psyxpert, BLUE-BOX) and multimodal
data-fusion strategies integrating physiological signals (EEG, ECG, GSR) with
visual and auditory data for emotion recognition and psychiatric monitoring.

Accepted 2026, in press.
