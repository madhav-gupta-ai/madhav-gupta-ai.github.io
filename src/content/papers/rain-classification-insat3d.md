Designed a Random Forest rainfall-classification model on INSAT-3D satellite
Outgoing Longwave Radiation (OLR) data, improving the Critical Success Index by
30.2% and the Equitable Threat Score by 30.9% over existing meteorological
methods.

Addressed severe class imbalance with targeted SMOTE oversampling and random
undersampling, improving recall by 56.1% while maintaining precision.
