Proposed a Random Forest Proximity-based Oversampling (RF-POS) technique that
outperformed SMOTE across 18 benchmark datasets (MCC 91.61% vs. 90.93%), with
Genetic Algorithms for synthetic-sample selection improving MCC by a further
2.09%.

Accepted 2025, in press. Placed 3rd in the Best Paper category at ICDMAI 2026.
